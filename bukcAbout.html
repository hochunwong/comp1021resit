British Universities Karting Championship

What is BUKC?

The British Universities Karting Championship (BUKC) is organised exclusively by Club100 Racing Ltd. It is a motor racing championship for currently enrolled students in higher education establishments in the United Kingdom.

The BUKC uses a unique format that uses teams of 4 drivers, and takes place over a number of rounds during the academic year at many top class karting venues throughout the UK. The championship is open to teams from any UK institution of higher education. Each university may be represented by more than one team of four drivers.

Over 1,000 Students from different universities across the UK compete each year!

Racing Licences & Test Days

Everyone who races in the BUKC needs a BUKC Race Licence. These are acquired at ‘Test Days’, sessions that run every year before the season begins.

There are usually three test days before the season begins.
