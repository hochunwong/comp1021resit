About Us

Welcome to Leeds University Union Motorsport!

The multi award-winning society bringing you karting, sim racing, F1 socials, and everything motorsport-related - we have something for all motorsport fans!

We at LUU Motorsport compete in the British Universities Karting Championship (BUKC), run our own in-house karting championship affectionately dubbed the Tour de Yorkshire, have a sim-racing hot lap challenge, host weekly socials to watch every Formula 1 race, and a whole lot more!

Karting

LUU Motorsport competes in the BUKC (British University Karting Championship), racing against over 50 other UK universities in karts capable of speeds of over 75mph and 5-60 in 4.5 seconds. There are 8 rounds at 4 tracks nationwide, plus several test-days throughout the year. The level of competition ranges from beginners trying BUKC for the first time to British Formula 3 race-winners across 4 competition tiers (Rookies, Intermediates, Clubmans, Premiers).

Typically consisting of 8 rounds, the Tour de Yorkshire takes members across Yorkshire to the crème de la crème of local indoor and outdoor karting circuits. This is the perfect place for beginners to try karting and hone their racing skills, you're guaranteed to see some fantastic racing all through the field!

Typically consisting of 3 rounds, this championship runs in the way same as Tour de Yorkshire but usually contains an hour long race that is split up amongst teammates. It creates a great social environment for you to create great friendships as well as developing your driving skills.

This event is similar to our TdY events. It is the perfect event for women to go karting whether you have experience or not. We run a women's only GiAG at the beginning of the year and numerous of events throughout the year. We have collaborated with universities across the UK to bring women in motorsports together for an amazing day of karting. If you are totally new to karting or just want to develop your skills in a women's only setting, this event is the best place to do so.

Socials

We run a vast variety of socials including our brand new bi-weekly sim-racing and hot lap challenges, fun nights out, quiz and game nights, car shows, and Formula 1 socials every race weekend! Perfect for anyone looking to meet new people with an interest in motorsport.
