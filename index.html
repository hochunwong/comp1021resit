We are the winners of the 2024 Gryphon Colours Club of the Year Award! Thank you so much to our wonderful committee, and to all of our members!

Things We Do

Karting

Not only do we host fortnightly karting championships (Tour de Yorkshire) and Endurance Championships (TdY Enduro), both with driver points/rankings, we also participate in the BUKC Club 100!

eTdY & Sim Racing

Physically limited no more! Join us in our digital eSports events as well! We host eTdY, a series where we challenge each other across different games such as Assetto Corsa, Dirt Rally 2, F1 2024, ...and much more!

Race Days

Come watch race days with us! Events we've visited in the past include British GT at Donington Park and British Super Bikes!

F1 Watchalongs

We watch F1 & other events together, and also play minigames for fun! Come play Guess the Grid and F1 Fantasy League with us, and join us for drinks and vieweings!

Socials

Party with us! Meet new people! But most importantly... have lots and lots of fun! Join us in events such as the Annual General Meeting, Racing Basics talk, guest talks, as well as party socials!

Rally Viewings

Get covered in mud! It's fun, trust me! Follow us and let's visit rally events like the Riponian Rally! Watch professional drivers roar past on a tight and dangerous path!
