Tour de Yorkshire

Welcome to our very own intra-society karting championship!

The Tour de Yorkshire typically consists of 8 rounds, taking you across local indoor and outdoor tracks in Yorkshire. Beginners are welcome and encouraged!

At the end of each year, the points scored by each driver are totalled up for positions!

Stay tuned on our WhatsApp channel for TdY announcements! Be quick, the tickets fly away fast! (just like us!)
